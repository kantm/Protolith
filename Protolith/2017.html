Protolith

Protolith originates from the amalgamation of two greek words- "Protos" meaning first and "lithos" meaning rock, thus Protolith stands for the parent or the original rock with this in mind, Protolith was launched as the biennial festival of Department of Earth Sciences at IIT Bombay in the year 2015. It is our immense pleasure to present to you Protolith 2017- the second edition of this festival.We promise to take Protolith to greater height and look forward to your participation.
